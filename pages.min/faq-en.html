Frequently Asked Questions

What is a validation?

A validation is a file-based check of an exported catalog file in CSV format.

What is delivered?

A PDF report and a CSV dataset with structural issues.

What is checked?

Structure, identifiers, format, roles, shares, encoding, and duplicates.

Is this a correction service?

No. Kataloghub performs validation only. Corrections are handled via HeyRoya.

Are files stored?

No. Files are not stored after delivery.

Is integration required?

No. No integration and no system access are required.
