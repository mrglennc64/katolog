Become a {{PartnerBrand}} partner

Five business days from agreement to your first live partner case. The path below is the same for every new partner.

Onboarding timeline

Each row is one calendar day. Day 0 starts when both sides sign the partner agreement.

Day 0

Agreement signed · brand assets requested

{{PartnerBrand}} sends a one-page intake form asking for logo, primary color, contact email, and the legal name to use on deliverables.

Day 1

Brand assets returned · onboarding kit prepared

Partner returns the intake form. {{PartnerBrand}} prepares a partner-specific onboarding kit with sample files and a checklist.

Day 2

Onboarding kit delivered

Onboarding kit delivered as a single zip. Partner walks through three sample cases internally — one CSV, one XLSX, one CWR — without sending real client data.

Day 3

Sample cases reviewed together

30-minute call to walk through any questions on the sample outputs. Partner confirms the brand styling on the deliverables looks right.

Day 4

First real case submitted

Partner forwards their first real end-client catalog. {{PartnerBrand}} processes as a normal case but flags it as "first" internally for extra review before delivery.

Day 5

First case delivered · partner is live

First case delivered to the partner. From this point on, every case follows the standard timing on the how it works page.

Materials needed from the partner

All collected on the Day 0 intake form. Nothing else is required.

  • Brand name — exact display form to use on every deliverable.
  • Logo — SVG preferred, PNG accepted. Light and dark variants if available.
  • Primary brand color — hex code. Used as the accent on Health Reports and Worksheets.
  • Partner contact email — the address shown to end-clients on every deliverable.
  • Support email — for ongoing case follow-up. Can be the same as the contact email.
  • Legal entity name + jurisdiction — for the wholesale invoice and the partner agreement.
  • Preferred language — English, Swedish, or both. Adds 1–2 days if a third language is required.

First-case checklist

Walk through this internally before sending the first real client catalog.

Before sending

  • All three sample cases (CSV, XLSX, CWR) processed end-to-end internally.
  • Brand styling on sample Health Report confirmed against the partner brand guide.
  • Partner-safe disclaimer text reviewed by the partner's legal contact.
  • End-client has agreed in writing to share the catalog file.

After delivery

  • Forward the Health Report and Worksheet to the end-client unchanged.
  • Schedule a 30-minute review call with the end-client before they fill in the Worksheet.
  • Wait for the completed Worksheet before initiating Part 2.
  • Log the case in the partner's own CRM for billing reconciliation.
