1. Definitions

  • {{PartnerBrand}} means the entity providing the service described in these terms.
  • Partner means the counterparty that has executed a partner agreement with {{PartnerBrand}}.
  • End-client means a customer of the Partner whose catalog is processed under the partner agreement. {{PartnerBrand}} has no direct contractual relationship with the End-client.
  • Catalog means a metadata file submitted by the Partner for processing.
  • Deliverable means any file produced by {{PartnerBrand}} and returned to the Partner: Health Report, Worksheet, corrected Catalog, or export template.
  • Case means a single end-to-end engagement on a Catalog from intake through final Deliverable.

2. Scope of service

{{PartnerBrand}} provides file-based catalog metadata validation and correction. The service consists of two phases: a Part 1 scan that produces a Health Report and Worksheet, and a Part 2 correction that produces a corrected Catalog and export templates.

The service is exclusively file-based. {{PartnerBrand}} does not access the Partner's systems, the End-client's systems, or any third-party platform. {{PartnerBrand}} does not act as a data ingestion pipeline, does not expose APIs to the Partner or the End-client, and does not provide a dashboard.

  3. The Partner must not represent {{PartnerBrand}} as having any direct relationship with the End-client.
  4. The Partner must not represent the service as offering live system access, royalty recovery, or any guaranteed financial outcome.

{{PartnerBrand}} grants the Partner a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to apply the Partner's own brand to all Deliverables produced under these terms, for the duration of the partner agreement. {{PartnerBrand}} retains no branding rights over the Deliverables once produced.

5. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to treat the other's non-public information as confidential and to use it only for the purpose of performing under these terms. This obligation survives termination for three years.

6. Data handling and retention

  1. Catalog files submitted by the Partner are retained for ninety (90) days from receipt to enable re-scan eligibility, then deleted from active storage.
  2. Aggregate counts (number of works scanned, number of issues identified) are retained indefinitely for billing reconciliation. These counts contain no personal data.
  3. {{PartnerBrand}} does not export, share, sell, or use the contents of any Catalog for any purpose other than producing Deliverables for the Partner.
  4. The Partner may request immediate deletion of any specific Catalog by written notice to {{PartnerContact}}. Deletion is completed within five business days.
