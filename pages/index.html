How the workflow runs

Six steps, all file-based. {{PartnerBrand}} stays the only name your end-clients see.

1

Partner receives a catalog file

Accepted formats: CSV, XLSX, CWR.

2

Part 1 scan

Identifier checks, role and share validation, duplicate and conflict detection.

3

Branded report + worksheet returned

Health report and worksheet are generated under {{PartnerBrand}} and sent back to the partner.

4

Partner returns the worksheet

With selected corrections confirmed by their client. No automatic changes.

5

Part 2 corrections applied

Only the rows the partner explicitly approved are written to the working catalog.

6

Branded corrected report + exports

A second health report, the corrected catalog, and the CWR/CSV export templates — all under {{PartnerBrand}}.
