What counts as a scan, a work, a case

Pricing units defined precisely so there are no billing surprises.

Scan

One Part 1 + Part 2 round-trip on a single catalog file. Re-scans of a re-submitted catalog within 30 days are not billed.

Work

One distinct musical or rights-bearing entry in the catalog. Multiple writers on the same work count as one work, not many.

Case

A single end-client engagement. One case may include multiple scans if the partner submits separate catalogs from the same client.

Billing terms

Currency
EUR. SEK or USD available on request for Standard and Volume tiers.
Invoicing
Monthly in arrears. Net 30. Direct to the partner — never to the end-client.
Minimum commitment
None on Starter. Standard and Volume require a 3-month initial term, then month-to-month.
Volume discounts
Apply automatically on Standard at 25 / 50 / 100 scans per month thresholds.
Retail markup
Set entirely by the partner. {{PartnerBrand}} does not see, set, or audit the retail price.
Payment methods
Bank transfer (IBAN). Card and SEPA on request.
