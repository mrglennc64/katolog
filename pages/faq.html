Frequently asked questions

Grouped by audience: partners considering the program, partners onboarding, and partners answering their own end-clients.

For partners considering {{PartnerBrand}}
Do I have to give {{PartnerBrand}} access to my systems?

No. The entire workflow is file-based. {{PartnerBrand}} never touches your systems, your end-client's systems, or any third-party platform. Files in, files out.

Will my end-clients know {{PartnerBrand}} is doing the work?

No, unless you tell them. Every deliverable is formatted under your brand. There is no {{PartnerBrand}} logo, mention, or watermark on any output. The contact email shown to end-clients is yours.

Do I have to commit to a minimum volume?

Not on the Starter tier — you pay per scan, no commitment. The Standard and Volume tiers have a 3-month initial term, then month-to-month. See the pricing page for details.

How long does it take to onboard?

Two to five business days. The onboarding kit (Standard and Volume tiers) includes sample files and a checklist you walk through internally before sending real client data.

About the workflow
What file formats do you accept?

CSV (UTF-8), XLSX (first worksheet), and CWR (v2.1 and v2.2). Full details on the how it works page.

What checks does the scan run?

Required identifier fields, valid roles, share-total math, duplicate works, and ownership conflicts between rows. The full rule list is in the reseller documentation (Standard tier and above).

What if my end-client refuses to confirm rows?

Nothing happens. The case stays open. {{PartnerBrand}} never applies a correction without an explicit "Accept" or "Edit" mark on the worksheet. A case can sit indefinitely.

Can I re-submit a corrected catalog for a free re-scan?

Yes — within 30 days of the original scan delivery, re-scans of the same catalog are not billed.

Branding and resale
Can I set my own retail price?

Yes — entirely. {{PartnerBrand}} bills you the wholesale rate and never sees, sets, or audits what you charge your end-clients.

Can I use my own logo and colors on every deliverable?

Yes. The Health Report, Worksheet headers, and export templates all carry your brand. We swap your logo, brand name, contact, and colors during onboarding.

Are there required disclaimers I have to show?

Yes — a short brand-neutral disclaimer must appear on every deliverable, stating that the analysis is metadata-only and does not constitute system access or any guarantee of financial outcome. Standard tier and above includes a ready-to-paste pack in your language.

Boundaries and what {{PartnerBrand}} does not do
Does {{PartnerBrand}} make API calls to STIM, ICE, or MLC?

No. {{PartnerBrand}} has no live system access of any kind. The workflow is entirely offline — file in, file out.

Does {{PartnerBrand}} promise royalty recovery or financial outcomes?

No. {{PartnerBrand}} validates and corrects metadata. Whether better metadata translates into better royalty outcomes is downstream — and entirely up to the partner's end-clients and the rights organizations they work with.

Will {{PartnerBrand}} ever change ownership data automatically?

No. Every change requires an explicit confirmation on the worksheet. If a row is unmarked or marked Reject, it is left untouched.

What happens to my client's data after a case closes?

Catalog files are retained for 90 days for re-scan eligibility, then deleted. Aggregate counts are kept for billing reconciliation. No personal data leaves the {{PartnerBrand}} environment.
